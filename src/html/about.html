About me

  • 1 March 2016
  • By Florian Dejonckheere

Hi there! My name is Florian, I am a student at HoGent in Ghent, Belgium. I like Belgian chocolate, Gueuze lambic-beer and riding my bike while I think about life the universe and everything. I have been fascinated by computers since I was little, and I made my first website at the age of 9. I switched to using Linux full-time when I was 13 years old, distrohopping from Ubuntu to Mandrake until I finally found the perfect distribution: Arch Linux. I am currently a maintainer in the Arch User Repository, where I maintain Arch Linux packages.
