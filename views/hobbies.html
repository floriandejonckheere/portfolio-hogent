Hobbies

Athletics

In my free time I practice athletics at my local athletics team. I've been doing this since I was little. When I say athletics, most people assume I just run laps, but in fact I'm a thrower. I practice shot put and discus throwing, two branches of athletics which in my opinion are underappreciated.

Music

I listen to a wide variation of music, from heavy metal to ambient to 1950s big band jazz. If you see me in public, chances are I have my music player with me and my IEMs in my ears.
I am currently in the process of developing an online platform that can predict and suggest music you might like based on a personalized profile of artists and albums you have given a rating. It uses a huge aggregated database of music and a set of complex predictive algorithms to determine your personal music taste.
